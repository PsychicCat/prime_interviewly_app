Welcome to Interviewly!

Hello!

What happens when you cross Sudoku, 'Speed Dating' and Interviewing? An event scheduling nightmare! Interviewly is here to help. Interviewly radically simplifies the process of scheduling events, specifically those difficult to manage events where you need to match up a bunch of interviewers with a bunch of interviewees in a short amount of time!

Using Interviewly is EASY!

Log In or Register to gain access to Interviewly.

Once you log in you are now on the Events page! If you have no Events click on Create New Event. To view an Event click on the Interviewly logo within the Event.

Click on Students to add new students to an Event. Click in Interviewers to add new interviewers to an Event. Each option allows you to add one at a time, in line, or in bulk via CSV upload!

Click on the Menu icon to view any Archived Events, your Profile where you can change your password, or Logout.

Once you have created an Event, added Students and added Interviewers click Generate and your Event will populate with everything you need!
